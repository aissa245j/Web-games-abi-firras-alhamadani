Frontier Supply Run

Overview

Run a convoy across hostile territory, trading with settlements and managing fuel, morale, and cargo space. Every round introduces a new hazard, and the market prices shift in response to what you sell.

Setup

  • Draw 12 route tiles (4 settlements, 4 hazards, 4 open routes). Shuffle and lay them in a 3x4 grid.
  • Start with 8 fuel, 4 morale, and 6 cargo slots.
  • Each settlement has 3 goods and 2 needs. Use tokens or a quick note.

Turn Structure

  • Plan: Spend 1 morale to reroute a single tile or scan 2 adjacent tiles.
  • Move: Spend 1 fuel to move to an adjacent tile. Hazards require a skill test.
  • Trade: At settlements, buy or sell goods. Prices shift if the market is flooded.
  • Resolve: Draw a weather or raider card that affects the next turn.

Win Condition

Reach the final settlement with at least 12 total value in cargo and 2 morale. For a hard mode, require 3 settlements visited and no route backtracking.
