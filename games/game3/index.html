Skyforge Fleet

Overview

Build a fleet of modular airships, manage supply lines, and engage in mission-driven battles across changing weather fronts.

Fleet Assembly

Each ship uses a hull plus up to 3 modules. Modules can be swapped between missions, but you pay upkeep for every active ship.

Scout Hull + Sensor Array
Destroyer Hull + Cannon Deck
Carrier Hull + Drone Bay
Support Hull + Repair Rig

Mission Flow

  • Draw 2 objectives and 1 weather front. Weather modifies range and visibility.
  • Allocate command points to move, attack, defend, or deploy drones.
  • Resolve combat by comparing attack strength to defense plus terrain bonuses.

Win Condition

Complete 3 objectives before your fleet morale drops to 0. For a campaign mode, carry over damaged modules between missions.
